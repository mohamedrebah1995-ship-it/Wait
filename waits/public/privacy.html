PRIVACY POLICY

Delivr — Driver Community · Last updated 5 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Delivr ("we", "us", "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application at drivers-eyes.web.app. By using Delivr you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information we collect

  • Account details: your driver name, email address, and (optionally) phone number and area/city.
  • Location data: with your permission, we use your device's GPS to show nearby restaurants, verify arrivals, and measure wait times. Location is used while the app is in use and is not sold.
  • Usage data: the restaurant wait times you log, your visit history, and messages you send in the driver chat.
  • Payment data: if you subscribe to Delivr Premium, payments are processed by Stripe. We never see or store your full card details.
  • Technical data: standard device and browser information, and cookies used by our advertising and analytics partners.

2. How we use your information

  • To provide core features: nearby restaurant discovery, arrival verification, and community wait-time data.
  • To operate the area-based driver chat.
  • To manage your account, subscription, and email verification.
  • To display advertising to free-tier users and to improve the app.

3. Advertising & cookies

Delivr uses Google AdSense to display ads to free users. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your activity. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. For more information about how Google uses data, see Google's Partner Sites policy.

Users in the EEA, UK, and Switzerland are shown a consent message to manage ad personalisation, as required by law. Delivr Premium subscribers do not see ads.

4. Third-party services

We rely on trusted providers who process data on our behalf:

  • Google Firebase — authentication and database hosting.
  • Google Maps / Places API — restaurant search and locations.
  • Google AdSense — advertising.
  • Stripe — subscription payment processing.
  • Brevo — sending verification emails.

5. Your rights

Under UK GDPR and applicable data protection law, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. You can edit your profile in-app, and you can request account deletion by contacting us. To stop location collection, disable location permission in your browser or device settings.

6. Data retention

We keep your account and log data while your account is active. Wait logs may be retained in anonymised, aggregated form to power community statistics. You can request deletion of your personal data at any time.

7. Children

Delivr is intended for delivery drivers aged 18 and over and is not directed at children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
